About Me

Data and Analytics Engineer with 5+ years of experience transforming raw data into strategic insights. I bridge the gap between technical execution and business outcomes, helping product teams make data-informed decisions that deliver real user value. My background spans machine learning, ETL pipelines, and full-stack development, but my true calling is using data to solve human-centered problems. I'm seeking international opportunities to bring my analytical expertise and cross-cultural perspective to teams building products for a global audience.

My approach: I believe in intentional analytics, understanding not just what the data says, but why it matters. I ask the right questions, connect metrics to user behavior, and translate findings into actionable product strategies.

Beyond the keyboard: When I'm not analyzing data, you'll find me exploring Bangkok's street food scene, practicing yoga, or learning languages. I believe curiosity is the best data scientist's tool and I apply that curiosity to everything I do.
